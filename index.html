ABOUT OUR COMPANY

TempCare has been proudly serving homes and businesses throughout the Chicagoland area since 2010. We are locally owned and operated, and we are deeply familiar with the unique heating and cooling needs of property owners in this area. We strive to exceed your expectations in all areas of HVAC service. We work 24/7 (even on weekends and holidays!) to ensure that your home and work environment are safe, clean, and comfortable, all year long.

When you call TempCare, you can be sure that you are working with an honest and ethical team of highly-skilled HVAC professionals. We also offer simple, hassle-free financing that makes HVAC installation and repair easier on your budget.

Examples of our most frequently requested services include air conditioning installation and repair, heating installation and repair, duct cleaning and repair, and other services that give your indoor air quality a boost.

With TempCare on your side, you can stay cool and breathe easier. Get back to your life, and leave the HVAC troubles to us!
