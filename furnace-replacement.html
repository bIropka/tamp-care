Is It Time to Replace Your Furnace?

If you’re not sure whether you should replace your furnace or not, look for these four indicators that it may be time:

  • Your furnace has reached the end of its useful life. That is, trying to make it run longer could become a safety hazard.
  • Your furnace is no longer keeping your home or business as warm as it used to.
  • You’re calling for furnace repairs more often than you used to, and those repairs are becoming more expensive every time.
  • Your heating bill has been increasing from year to year. Perhaps it’s now the highest it has ever been.
