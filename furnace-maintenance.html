Your A/C was a Big Investment, and You Want to Keep it in Great Shape.

Like a car, your air conditioner runs best when it gets the attention it deserves. Proper A/C maintenance ensures that the unit will offer peak performance all year long. It can extend the life of your air conditioner, reduce energy costs, and more. So, don’t ignore your routine air conditioner service needs. You’ll notice a major difference after it has been serviced.

A/C Care and Maintenance Facts You Should Know

  • Almost all air conditioner failures are the result of neglecting system maintenance.
  • Dirty air conditioning units take two times longer to cool a space than a well-maintained unit.
  • Also, because it works so much harder, a dirty a/c will cost you about 50% more to operate.
  • A well-maintained air conditioner can last you as long as 15-20 years! A neglected unit will fail several years earlier.

5 benefits of routine A/C maintance

Greater
comfort

Having your air conditioning system break down in the middle of the summer heat is something you want to avoid. The best way to keep the unit running is by conducting routine maintenance. This will keep your home comfortable even when it is hot outside.

Fewer
repairs

It will cost you less to replace a pump or A/C belt than it would to have to replace the entire unit because of severe damage. One of the benefits of air conditioner maintenance is being able to locate problems and repair them before they become larger issues.

Improved
air
quality

Dirty components and clogged air filters are just some of the things that can cause poor air quality in your home, resulting in allergies and other breathing problems. Improved air quality is one of the best benefits of air conditioning maintenance.

Monthly
savings

An air conditioner that runs efficiently costs less to operate than one that is having problems. When the unit is under stress from malfunctioning parts or worn belts, it takes more power to operate, costing you more on your energy bill.

Longer
system
life

Regular ac maintenance on your system will prolong the life of your air conditioning unit by preventing major breakdowns. Checking the A/C system for any wear on the pump, belts and other parts allows you to keep the system running at peak performance.
